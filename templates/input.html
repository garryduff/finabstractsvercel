Abstract

This app allows you to predict what journal a paper could be published in, based on just its abstract. It uses the same software as ChatGPT, fine-tuned on the abstracts of thousands of academic journals. Based on the text structure of each abstract, a prediction is made about the most suitable publication destinations.
