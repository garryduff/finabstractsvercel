Predictor

This app allows you to predict what journal a paper could be published in, based on just its abstract. To try it, paste in an abstract, and then press Evaluate.



Result

This abstracts scores 4.9 out of a maximum of 5.0. Other abstracts of this score have had a 76.0% success rate of being published in a world elite (4+ star) journal, and 89.2% of being published in a leading (4 star) journal. The most likely venues for publication are Journal of Finance, Review of Financial Studies, and Journal of Financial Economics
